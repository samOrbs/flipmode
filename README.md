# Flipmode - BJJ Research Assistant

Get personalized BJJ research from your coach, delivered directly to your Obsidian vault.

---

## Athlete Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Install Obsidian

Download Obsidian (free):
- **iPhone/iPad:** [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/obsidian/id1557175442)
- **Android:** [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=md.obsidian)
- **Desktop:** [obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md)

### Step 2: Create Your Vault

1. Open Obsidian
2. Tap **Create new vault**
3. Name it `Flipmode`
4. Tap **Create**

### Step 3: Install the Plugin

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Tap **Community plugins**
3. Turn OFF **Restricted mode** (tap OK to confirm)
4. Tap **Browse** → search for **BRAT** → **Install** → **Enable**
5. Go back to **Community plugins** → tap **BRAT**
6. Tap **Add Beta plugin**
7. Enter: `samOrbs/flipmode`
8. Tap **Add Plugin**
9. Go back to **Community plugins** → Enable **Flipmode**

### Step 4: Connect to Your Coach

1. **Get your token** from your coach (via Discord message)
2. Go to **Settings** → **Flipmode**
3. Set **Mode** to `Remote (Athlete)`
4. Paste your **Athlete Token**
5. Tap outside the box to save

You're ready!

---

## Submitting a Question

### Voice Note (Recommended)

1. Open command palette (swipe down or tap ⌘ icon)
2. Type **Voice** and tap **Flipmode: Voice Note**
3. Tap **Record** and talk about what happened in training
4. Tap **Stop** when done
5. The AI asks 2-3 quick questions to understand your situation
6. Your refined question is sent to your coach

### Text Query

1. Open command palette
2. Type **Submit** and tap **Flipmode: Submit Query**
3. Type your question
4. Submit

---

## Getting Research Back

1. Open command palette
2. Type **Sync** and tap **Flipmode: Sync from Coach**
3. Your research appears in `Flipmode/Research/`

Check back periodically - your coach will process your questions.

---

## Listening to Research

Use your phone's built-in text-to-speech:

**iPhone/iPad:**
1. Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content
2. Turn ON **Speak Selection**
3. In Obsidian, select text → tap **Speak**

**Android:**
1. Settings → Accessibility → Select to Speak → ON
2. In Obsidian, select text → tap play button

## Tips for Better Questions

**Be specific about what happened:**
> "I was in bottom half guard and they smashed my frames and passed"

**Mention the position:**
> "From closed guard when they stand up..."

**Describe what you tried:**
> "I tried to hip escape but they had my head controlled"

The AI therapist will help clarify before sending to your coach.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Cannot connect to server" | Check internet, verify token is correct |
| "No completed research" | Coach hasn't finished yet - check later |
| Plugin not showing | Reload plugins in Community plugins settings |
| Voice note fails | Check microphone permissions for Obsidian |
